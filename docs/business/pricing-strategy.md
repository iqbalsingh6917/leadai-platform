## Pricing Rationale

### Why so low vs. competition?

1. **India-First**: HubSpot charges ₹4,000+/mo for basic features. We deliver more for ₹499.
2. **Low overhead**: AI automation reduces our support and operations cost by 60%.
3. **Volume strategy**: High volume at low price → data advantage → better AI → better product → defensibility.
4. **Trust first**: Low margins early = faster customer acquisition = word-of-mouth growth.
