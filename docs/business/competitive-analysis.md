3. **💬 WhatsApp-First for India**: 600M+ Indian WhatsApp users. We treat WhatsApp as a primary channel, not an afterthought.

4. **⚡ 15-Minute Onboarding**: LeadAI vs. 3+ weeks for HubSpot, 2+ months for Salesforce. No consultants needed.

5. **🇮🇳 India-Native UX**: UPI payments, Indian language support, INR pricing, Indian business formats (GST invoices).

6. **💰 10x Lower Price Point**: Professional features at ₹1,999/mo vs. ₹33,000+/mo for HubSpot Professional.

7. **🧠 Budget Optimizer**: Automated ad spend optimization across Meta, Google, and more. No competitor offers this at this price.

8. **🔓 No Lock-In**: Open standards, full data export, API access. Competitors make leaving painful.
